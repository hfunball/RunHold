Voice

  • Use direct, specific utility language.
  • Talk about games and simple personal Windows workflows.
  • Keep descriptions short, practical, and concrete.
  • Use “holding keys” and “release all” consistently.

Avoid

  • Long explanations in product surfaces.
  • Claims about broad game compatibility before testing.
  • Visual choices that feel loud, edgy, or hard to publish.
  • Positioning that makes the app sound bigger than it is.
